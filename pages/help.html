Ordering & Custom Requests

How do I place a custom order? expand_more

For custom commissions (like specific colors, sizes, or designs not listed), please reach out to me directly via Instagram or Facebook Messenger. We'll discuss your design, timeline, and pricing before you place a deposit.

How long does it take to make an item? expand_more

Production time varies depending on the complexity of the item and my current workload (as I am a full-time student). Small items like keychains may take 1-3 days, while larger items like cardigans or plushies can take 1-2 weeks. I will provide an estimated completion date when you order.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept? expand_more

We primarily accept GCash for downpayments. The remaining balance can be paid via GCash or Cash on Delivery (COD) during the meet-up.

Do I need to pay a downpayment? expand_more

Yes, a 50% non-refundable downpayment is required for all custom orders and reserved items. This covers the cost of materials and secures your slot in my queue.

Product Care

How do I wash my crochet items? expand_more
  • Hand Wash (Recommended): Soak in cool water with mild detergent. Gently squeeze (do not wring). Lay flat to dry on a towel.
  • Machine Wash: Place in a mesh laundry bag and use the delicate/gentle cycle with cold water. Air dry only (no dryer).
